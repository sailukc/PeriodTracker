EXISTING SYSTEM VS PROPOSED SYSTEM

Existing System:
- Many users track their periods manually using calendars or notes.
- Some mobile apps exist but many do not offer AI-based predictions.
- Limited or no forecasting of symptoms and mood.
- Little or no personalized health insights.
- No integrated chatbot to answer menstrual health questions.
- Data is often locked and not customizable or open-source.

Proposed System (Period Tracker):
- Web-based period tracking platform with AI-powered predictions.
- Predicts next period, cycle length, fertile window, and ovulation day.
- Predicts likely symptoms and mood for a given day of the cycle.
- Generates personalized health insights based on user history.
- Provides a Flo-style AI chatbot for interactive support.
- Built using HTML/CSS/JS on the frontend and Django + ML models on the backend.
- Customizable, educational, and suitable for academic research and extension.
